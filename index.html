Ending Covid-19’s Adversity

Safety Precautionary measures for Covid-19

Eat healthy!

The most prescribed foods in fighting Covid 19 are fresh and rich in vitamins, minerals, dietary fibre, protein and antioxidants that will strengthen the immune system. www.emro.who.int

Tips for healthy eating

Drink water!

For water intake a day adult male are expected to drink 3.7 liters (15 cups) and 2.7 liters (11cups) for female adult. www.onemedical.com

Why drink water?

Let hand sanitation be a routine!

Frequent hand washing and applying of alcohol or alcohol-based sanitizers protect one’s life from the threat of Covid-19 and to practice proper health behaviour. https://www.cdc.gov/handwashing/when-how-handwashing.html

Proper Handwashing

Get an adequate Sleep!

Sleep is vital in one’s health and well-being. It is a way of repairing and preparing body at the same time. www.sleepfoundation.org

Engage in physical activities!

Pumping iron, keeping fit and working-out are all necessary in maintaining and boosting up an immune system and well-being in general especially in times of a pandemic like Covid-19. https://patients.healthquest.org/exercise-is-essential-for-well-being-during-covid-19-pandemic/

Be germfree! Be hygienic!

A hygienic practice lower the risk of being infected and adapting a disease. It is beneficial to one’s personal health and so is to other people. www.health.gov.au

Mask is a must!

Wearing a mask alone can’t help someone avoid the Covid-19. But wearing a mask can help someone to stop the transmission of Covid-19. https://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019/question-and-answers-hub/q-a-detail/q-a-on-covid-19-and-masks

Keep Distance!

Practice 1m (3 feet) social distancing to limit the widespread of Covid-19! https://www.who.int/westernpacific/emergencies/covid-19/information/physical-distancing

Stay Indoors!

Acting as a homebody for the meantime will help lessen the increasing cases of Covid-19. https://covid19.healthypilipinas.ph

Wipe, Clean and Disinfect!

Germs can be indivisibly transmitted and so Covid-19. Therefore cleaning and disinfecting environmental surfaces is one way of supressing Covid-19 transmission. https://www.avidbots.com/avidbots-blog-covid-19-surface-life-and-the-importance-of-disinfecting/

Practice coughing etiquette!

Covering nose and mouth with tissues and mask on will stop the spread of germs. Stay away from people when coughing or sneezing. Be responsible enough and keep the hands clean. https://www.cdc.gov/healthywater/hygiene/etiquette/coughing_sneezing.html
